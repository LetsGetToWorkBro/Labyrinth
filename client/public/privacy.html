Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Labyrinth BJJ ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information through the Labyrinth BJJ Member App ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account information: Email address, full name, phone number
  • Membership data: Belt rank, membership type, enrollment date
  • Training history: Class check-ins, attendance timestamps, class names
  • Achievements and progress: Badge unlocks, streak data, game scores
  • Payment information: Card brand, last 4 digits, and expiration date (full card numbers are never stored — processed by Stripe)
  • Communications: Messages sent in the team chat
  • Waiver signatures: Digital signature image and acceptance timestamp
  • Family member data: Names and belt ranks of minor members linked to a parent account, collected through in-person enrollment with parental consent

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • Display your training stats, schedule, and achievements
  • Process membership payments via Stripe
  • Generate Apple Wallet and Google Wallet membership passes
  • Send class schedule updates and gym announcements
  • Enable door access at Labyrinth BJJ locations

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Sheets via Google Apps Script on Google's infrastructure. Payment data is processed and stored by Stripe. Session tokens are stored locally on your device.

4. Third Parties

We share data with the following third parties only as necessary to operate the App:

  • Google (GAS / Sheets / YouTube): Backend database, live class streaming
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Apple: Apple Wallet pass delivery
  • Google Wallet: Google Wallet pass delivery
  • Cloudflare: App hosting and CDN
  • Railway: Wallet pass generation server
  • Sentry: Error tracking (no personally identifiable information is intentionally sent)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adults 18 and older. Minor members (under 18) are enrolled through a parent or guardian account. Data for minor members is collected only through the in-person enrollment process with explicit parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from children independently, do not use minor member data for advertising, and do not share it beyond the third parties listed above. Parents may request deletion of their child's data by contacting us.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Member data is retained for the duration of your membership and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and administrative purposes. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at info@labyrinth.vision with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed before storage. Session tokens are stored locally and cleared on logout. We do not store full payment card numbers.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at info@labyrinth.vision.
